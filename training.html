ABOUT US













Maybelline is an American beauty brand founded in Chicago in 1914 and is
                 known for manufacturing and delivering top-notch cosmetics, 
                 fragrances, and personal care products. It’s worth mentioning 
                 that since 1996 it has been a subsidiary of the prominent 
                 French cosmetics company L’Oréal.
In India, this cosmetic company made its footprint in 1998, 
        and since then, it has risen to the apex, and all credit for 
        this goes to the brand’s affordable product line made exclusively 
        for the Indian consumer. Apart from gold-standard Maybelline cosmetics and 
        beauty items like – colour palettes, foundations, lipsticks, and eye makeup. ...Read more
